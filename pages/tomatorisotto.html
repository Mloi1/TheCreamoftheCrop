Vegan Tomato Risotto with courgettes

Ingredients

Fresh tomatoes,diced

Arborio rice, 1 cup

Tomato puree, 1 tsp

Vegetable stock (either made with stock or veggie scraps), 6 cups

1 onion

3 garlic cloves*

3 tbsp olive oil

fresh basil leaves

salt and pepper to taste

courgette, 1, shredded lengthways


* we love garlic and always chuck in a few extra cloves for good measure!



Instructions

1. Saute the onion and garlic

2. Add the risotto rice and cook, stirring constantly to avoid burning. Allow to cook for about 2 to 3 minutes.

3. Add 1 cup of vegetable broth and stir well to combine. When most of the liquid has been absorbed, add one more cup of water, along with the tomatoes, basil, and parsley.

4. Add salt and pepper, to taste

5. As the moisture gets absorbed, continue to add broth or water one cup at a time, stirring frequently, until the rice is cooked.

6. Meanwhile, heat a frying pan and lightly saute the shredded courgettes, flavouring with salt, pepper and chilli flakes

7. Serve in your favourite bowl and enjoy!
