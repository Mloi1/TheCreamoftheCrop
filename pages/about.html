About Cream of the Crop

This website is for anyone interested in eating healthily and trying new recipes.

We aim to create an active online community, and this is a platform which will share recipes and dishes created by our community.

Feel free to follow the recipes on here, adapt them, or make your own.

Share your creations with us, as winners will be in for a tasty surprise!



About Us

We are Martina, Holly and Maya - interested in inspiring others to eat healthily.

We want to show you that eating healthy doesn't have to be expensive.

The recipes shared on our website by us will largely be meat-free, but we welcome all recipe submissions, as long as they are healthy!
