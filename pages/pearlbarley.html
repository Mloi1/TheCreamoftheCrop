Pearl Barley with aubergine tomato sauce, roasted vegetables and scrambled eggs

Ingredients (doses for 1 person)

  • Pearl Barley 80g
  • Aubergine 1/2
  • Tomato sauce
  • Veggies of your choice
  • Egg 1 or 2
  • Salt, pepper and basil

Instructions

1. Cut the aubergine and add to the tomato sauce in a pan and place on the hob.
2. Bring the tomato sauce to boil, then reduce the flame and let simmer until the aubergines are ready. For a tastier tomato sauce, add onion, salt and olive oil.
3. Bring water to boil in another pan and add the barley. Let it simmer until ready, then drain.
4. Cut the rest of the vegetables and place in a frying pan with some olive oil.
5. Stir fry your vegetables, then break an egg and keep stirring until ready.
6. Place the barley and vegetables in a plate, add some basil and pepper and serve hot.
7. Enjoy!
