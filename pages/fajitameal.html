Fun Fajita meal

Ingredients

Avocadoes, 2

Peppers, 3 of your favourite colours

Sweet potato, 1

Any other veggies of your choice

Vegetable oil

Onions, 2

3 garlic cloves*

fajita wraps

spicy rice

salt and pepper to taste

chilli flakes

herbs of your choice


* we love garlic and always chuck in a few extra cloves for good measure!



Instructions

1. Chop all the veggies and pre-heat the oven

2. Place the veggies on a baking tray, coat with oil, and add herbs, salt and pepper

3. When the veggies are done, plate them up!

4. Microwave the spicy rice (sorry - easy route!)

5. Microwave the fajitas for 10 seconds, or pop them into the oven before serving.

6. Have fun assembling your fajita wraps!
